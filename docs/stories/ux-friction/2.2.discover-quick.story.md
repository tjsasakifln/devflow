# Story 2.2: Discover Quick — 3 Relatórios Essenciais

**Epic:** UX Friction Reduction — Devflow Onboarding
**Sprint:** 2 (Médio Prazo)
**Priority:** P1 — Alta
**Effort:** 5 pontos
**Status:** Done

## Problem Statement

`devflow discover` executa pipeline de 5 fases (scout, archaeologist, detective, architect, writer) gerando ~13 relatórios. Usuário leigo vê a lista e desiste de ler. Não há priorização, sumário executivo, nem modo rápido.

## User Story

AS a developer joining an existing codebase
I WANT `devflow discover --quick` to give me 3 essential reports
SO THAT I can understand the codebase in 10 minutes instead of 2 hours

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `devflow discover --quick` generates exactly 3 reports:
   - **Mapa do Sistema** (`system-map.md`): estrutura de diretórios, entry points, dependências principais, stack
   - **Mapa de Riscos** (`risk-map.md`): top 10 riscos técnicos (circular deps, god objects, segurança)
   - **Zonas de Mudança** (`change-zones.md`): safe/green, caution/yellow, danger/red zones
2. Full discover pipeline still available via `devflow discover` (no flag) or `devflow discover --full`
3. `--quick` output ends with executive summary (max 1 page)
4. Each of the 3 reports is self-contained (no cross-references)
5. Phase names aliased from metaphors to verbs (scout→scan, archaeologist→analyze, etc.)
6. `--phase=<name>` accepts both old and new names (backward compat)
